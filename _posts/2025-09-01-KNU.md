We are excited to announce that we have joined KNU CSE and launched the CVLab.
We're looking for impressive and self-motivated students to join us!
At the KNU CVLab, we pioneer next-generation technologies designed to advance visual intelligence and deep learning. 
Our core focus lies in reasoning about relationships between objects within images.
Also, we have interest in Neuro-symbolic learning and Physical AI.
Of course, we welcome discussions on any cutting-edge Computer Vision topics. 
Contact us with your CV. Let's shape the future of Computer Vision together.
